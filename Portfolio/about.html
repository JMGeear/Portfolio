Jeff Geear

A Programmer's Portfolio

My name is Jeff Geear and I was born in Toronto, Canada. I am an aspiring programmer and one of the lead singers and guitarists for the rock band Viraltone.

My computer abilities to troubleshoot hardware and software problems, work as a team player, and meet deadlines combined with my attention to detail would contributed to my success. I possess web programming skills, Java programming skills, DB2, C#, MySQL, PHP, skills in business writing, an outstanding knowledge of Microsoft Office including Project and Visio. I am skilled at working with others to provide information and solutions to potential and existing issues. My outgoing and friendly nature allows me to interact well with other individuals at all levels and I am able to bridge the gap between technical and non-technical persons.

I was introduced to music at an early age and after a couple of years taking Piano lessons I switched to guitar. My father gave me my first guitar which was a beat up six string acoustic with a really warped neck. I studied music at the Carlaw Conservatory of Music for five years. I remember when I bought my first guitar. It was a black Washburn electric, which I still have and play today. That guitar never left my side. I played in bands after High school but we eventually ended up going our separate ways. My interest in computers began with a Commodore 64. It wasn’t until I attended college that I really began to use computers and realized the limitless potential of the computer age.
